History of METPALLY DEPOT

The Metpally bus depot, part of the Telangana State Road Transport Corporation (TSRTC), operates under the TSRTC, which was formed in 2016 after
Telangana separated from Andhra Pradesh. The TSRTC itself has roots in the Nizam State Rail & Road Transport established in 1932 . While specific
details about the Metpally depot's exact establishment date are not readily available, it's understood to be part of the TSRTC's broader infrastructure.
